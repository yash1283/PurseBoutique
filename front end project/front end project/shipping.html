Shipping & Returns

Shipping Information

Shipping Options

At PurseBoutique, we offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Cost
Standard Shipping 5-7 business days $5.95 (FREE on orders over $75)
Expedited Shipping 2-3 business days $12.95
Express Shipping 1-2 business days $19.95
International Shipping 10-14 business days Varies by location

Delivery times are estimates and begin from the date of shipping, not the date of order.

Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed after 1:00 PM EST may not be processed until the next business day. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order has shipped.

International Shipping

We ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight.

Please note that international orders may be subject to:

  • Customs duties and taxes
  • Import fees
  • Customs clearance delays

These fees are not included in the purchase price and are the responsibility of the recipient. We cannot predict which orders will incur these additional charges as they vary by country.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by:

  • Logging into your account and viewing your order history
  • Using the "Track Order" feature on our website
  • Contacting our customer service team

Shipping Restrictions

We are unable to ship to P.O. boxes for expedited and express shipping methods. We also cannot ship to certain international locations due to shipping restrictions. If you're unsure if we ship to your location, please contact customer service before placing an order.

Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery under the following conditions:

  • Items must be unused, unworn, and in the original condition
  • All tags and original packaging must be intact
  • Proof of purchase is required (order number, receipt, or confirmation email)

Items Not Eligible for Return

The following items cannot be returned unless they are defective:

  • Personalized or customized items
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Gift cards
  • Intimate accessories (cosmetic bags, purse organizers, etc.)
  • Items damaged due to customer misuse

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return:

  1. Log into your account and go to "Order History"
  2. Find the order containing the item(s) you wish to return
  3. Click "Return Items" and follow the instructions
  4. Print the prepaid return shipping label
  5. Package the item(s) securely in original packaging if possible
  6. Attach the return label and drop off at the specified carrier

If you don't have an account or need assistance, contact our customer service team for help.

Return Shipping

For domestic returns, we provide a prepaid return shipping label. A fee of $6.95 will be deducted from your refund to cover the return shipping cost, unless the return is due to our error or a defective item.

For international returns, customers are responsible for return shipping costs and must use a trackable shipping method.

Refund Process

Once we receive your return, it will be inspected within 3-5 business days. If approved, your refund will be processed and automatically applied to your original payment method. Please allow 5-10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement, depending on your payment provider's policies.

You will receive an email notification when your return has been processed.

Exchanges

We are happy to exchange items for a different size, color, or style. To request an exchange, follow the same process as initiating a return, but select "Exchange" instead of "Return" and specify the item you'd like in exchange.

If the new item costs more than the original, you'll be charged the difference. If it costs less, you'll receive a refund for the difference.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery. Provide your order number and photos of the damage. We'll arrange for a return, replacement, or refund at no cost to you.
