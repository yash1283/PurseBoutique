Frequently Asked Questions

ROVOK is an online retailer specializing in high-quality, affordable luxury handbags and accessories. We curate a selection of stylish, contemporary designs that combine fashion-forward aesthetics with practical functionality.

Creating an account is easy! Simply click on the user icon in the top navigation bar and select "Sign Up". Fill in your details, create a password, and you're all set. Having an account allows you to track orders, save your favorite items, and enjoy a faster checkout process.

You can reach our customer service team through multiple channels:
  • Email us at rovok@support.com
  • Call us at (+91) 9996273595 during business hours
  • Fill out the contact form on our Contact Page
  • Live chat available on our website during business hours

Currently, rovok operates exclusively online. This allows us to keep our overhead costs low and pass those savings on to our customers. We do occasionally participate in pop-up shops and fashion events - follow our social media channels for announcements about these opportunities to see our products in person.

Yes, all of our products are 100% authentic. We source directly from manufacturers and authorized distributors. We do not sell counterfeit or replica items. Each bag comes with authenticity documentation and our quality guarantee.

We use a variety of high-quality materials including full-grain leather, vegan leather alternatives, canvas, and specialty fabrics. Each product page provides detailed information about the specific materials used in that item. We prioritize both quality and sustainability in our material selection.

Care instructions vary based on the material of your bag. Generally:
  • Leather bags: Clean with a soft, damp cloth and use a leather conditioner occasionally. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight or rain.
  • Canvas bags: Spot clean with mild soap and water. Some canvas bags are machine washable (check product details).
  • Vegan leather: Wipe clean with a damp cloth and mild soap.
Specific care instructions are included with each purchase and on individual product pages.

Yes, we offer monogramming services for select items. Look for the "Personalization Available" badge on eligible products. We can add initials or small custom details to these bags for an additional fee. Please note that personalized items cannot be returned unless there is a manufacturing defect.

Placing an order is simple:
  1. Browse our products and select the item(s) you want to purchase
  2. Choose your preferred color and quantity
  3. Click "Add to Cart"
  4. Review your cart and click "Proceed to Checkout"
  5. Enter your shipping and payment information
  6. Review your order and click "Place Order"
You'll receive an order confirmation email shortly after placing your order.

We accept multiple payment methods including:
  • Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • Shop Pay
  • Afterpay/Klarna (for orders over $50)
All transactions are secure and encrypted.

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it, provided it hasn't been processed yet. To do so, log into your account and go to "Order History", or contact our customer service team immediately. Once an order has been processed or shipped, it cannot be modified or canceled, but you can return it according to our return policy.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can click the tracking link in the email or enter the tracking number on our website in the "Order Tracking" section. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

We offer several shipping options:
  • Standard Shipping (5-7 business days): $5.95 or FREE on orders over $75
  • Expedited Shipping (2-3 business days): $12.95
  • Express Shipping (1-2 business days): $19.95
  • International Shipping (10-14 business days): Varies by location
Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and chosen shipping method.

Order processing typically takes 1-2 business days before your order ships. After shipping, delivery times depend on your chosen shipping method:
  • Standard: 5-7 business days
  • Expedited: 2-3 business days
  • Express: 1-2 business days
  • International: 10-14 business days (may be subject to customs delays)
These are estimated timeframes and may vary based on location and external factors like weather or carrier delays.

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight. Please note that international orders may be subject to customs duties, taxes, or import fees, which are the responsibility of the recipient. Delivery times for international orders typically range from 10-14 business days but may take longer depending on customs processing.

If your package is lost or arrives damaged, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of the delivery date (or expected delivery date for lost packages). Provide your order number and photos of any damage. We'll work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue and arrange for a replacement or refund as appropriate. All packages are insured during transit for your protection.

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in original, unused condition with all tags attached and original packaging intact. Personalized items, final sale items, and certain accessories cannot be returned unless defective. Please visit our Shipping & Returns page for complete details.

To initiate a return:
  1. Log into your account and go to "Order History"
  2. Find the order containing the item(s) you wish to return
  3. Click "Return Items" and follow the instructions
  4. Print the prepaid return shipping label
  5. Package the item(s) securely in original packaging if possible
  6. Attach the return label and drop off at the specified carrier
You can also initiate a return by contacting our customer service team.

Once we receive your return, it typically takes 3-5 business days to inspect and process. After processing, refunds are issued to your original payment method. Credit card refunds usually appear on your statement within 5-10 business days, while other payment methods may vary. You'll receive an email notification when your refund has been processed.

Yes, we offer exchanges for different sizes, colors, or styles. To request an exchange, follow the same process as initiating a return, but select "Exchange" instead of "Return" and specify the item you'd like in exchange. If the new item costs more than the original, you'll be charged the difference. If it costs less, you'll receive a refund for the difference.
